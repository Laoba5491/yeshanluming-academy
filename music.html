音樂之鄉Land of Music

音樂，是人類靈魂深處最動人的聲音。

本欄目不以播放音樂為主，因為這個世界從不缺少音樂平台。我們真正希望介紹的，是音樂背後的人。

那些偉大的音樂家、作曲家、歌手與演奏者，他們如何在苦難、孤獨、戰爭、愛情與命運之中，創作出震撼人心的不朽傳世作品；那些流傳世界的旋律背後，又隱藏著怎樣的人生故事與時代特色。

從貝多芬，到阿炳；從西方古典音樂，到中國傳統名曲。本欄目將與讀者一起，不只「聽見音樂」，而是「理解音樂」。

因為真正偉大的音樂，往往也是人類靈魂的歷史。

Land of Music音樂之鄉

Music is the most moving voice from the depths of the human soul.

This section is not centered on playing music, because the world already has no shortage of music platforms. What we truly hope to introduce are the people behind the music.

These great musicians, composers, singers, and performers — how they created immortal masterpieces amid suffering, loneliness, war, love, and fate; and what kinds of life stories and historical spirit lie hidden behind those melodies that have echoed across the world.

From Beethoven to Blind Musician Abing; from Western classical music to traditional Chinese masterpieces, this section hopes to guide readers not merely to “hear music,” but to “understand music.”

For truly great music is often also the history of the human soul.
