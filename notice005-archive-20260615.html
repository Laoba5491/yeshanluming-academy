野山鹿鳴書院網站今日正式上線，目前網站仍處於建設與測試初期階段，本次屬於第一次測試性上線發佈。 目前已上線內容，僅包括《小說天地》欄目中的《微型小說》系列，共十篇作品，供讀者閱讀與測試使用。 未來將陸續推出更多小說作品，以及下列欄目的系列內容： 寶石世界欄目 音樂之鄉欄目 中國歷史回顧欄目 歐洲歷史啟示欄目 感謝您的耐心與支持， 敬請期待更多精彩內容。 作者 野山鹿鳴 敬上 野山鹿鳴書院創辦人
YeshanLuming Academy officially opens today. At present, the website is still in its early stage of development and testing. This release represents the Academy’s first public test launch. The content currently available online consists of the Micro Stories series in the Fiction section, featuring a total of ten works for readers to enjoy and help evaluate during the testing period. In the future, additional fiction works will be published, together with new content from the following sections: The World of Gemstones The Land of Music Reflections on Chinese History Lessons from European History As the Academy continues to grow, more series and works will gradually be added. Thank you for your patience and support. We look forward to sharing many more stories, articles, and discoveries with you in the future. With best wishes, Yeshan Luming Founder and Author YeshanLuming Academy
