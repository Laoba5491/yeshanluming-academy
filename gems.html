寶石世界Realm of Gems

寶石，不只是財富與裝飾。

每一顆寶石背後，都藏著地球極深處，億萬年的機緣巧合，萬里挑一機會孕育，也藏著人類文明、王朝興衰、愛情、欲望、戰爭與傳奇。

本欄目包括鑽石、紅寶石、藍寶石、祖母綠、翡翠及珍珠等系列文章，既有嚴肅認真的寶石學知識介紹，又有寶石歷史故事傳奇，與讀者一起輕鬆進入神秘的寶石世界。

作者野山鹿鳴係資深寶石學家，擁有美國寶石學院 GG 寶石學家頭銜，及多年寶石從業研究經驗。

此外，亦收錄作者《羅浮宮珠寶大劫案》等結合歷史、藝術與珠寶傳奇的其它專題作品。

這裡不只是介紹寶石，也是透過寶石，探視人類文明與命運的一面鏡子。

Realm of Gems寶石世界

Gems are not merely symbols of wealth and ornament.

Behind every gemstone lies an almost impossible miracle born deep within the earth over hundreds of millions of years. Hidden within them are also the stories of human civilization, the rise and fall of dynasties, love, desire, war, and legend.

This section includes series devoted to diamonds, rubies, sapphires, emeralds, jadeite, pearls, and more. Alongside serious and carefully researched gemological knowledge, readers will also find historical stories and gemstone legends, entering together into the mysterious world of gems.

The author, Yeshan Luming, is a senior gemologist who holds the Graduate Gemologist (GG) title from the Gemological Institute of America (GIA), with many years of professional experience and research in the gem trade.

This section also includes other special works by the author, such as The Great Louvre Jewelry Heist, combining history, art, and the legends of jewels.

Here, gemstones are not merely introduced as objects of beauty, but as mirrors reflecting human civilization and destiny.
