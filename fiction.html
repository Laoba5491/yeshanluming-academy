這裡，不只是小說。 有歷史深處的嘆息，有人性幽暗中的微光，也有亂世裡依然不肯熄滅的真情與理想。 本欄目收錄作者野山鹿鳴創作的各類文學作品，包括微型小說、中篇小說、長篇小說。有的來自現實人生，有的來自歷史長河，有的來自作者對人性、命運與時代的長久思考。 這裡既有溫柔，也有殘酷；既有繁華如夢，也有黑暗深淵。 願讀者能從故事中，洞察人間，引起深思，。。。。。。

Fiction Garden小說天地

This is more than a collection of stories. Here, you will hear the sighs of history, glimpse the faint light of humanity shining through its darkest moments, and discover love, hope, and ideals that refuse to be extinguished, even in the midst of turbulent times. This section presents the literary works of Yeshan Luming, including flash fiction, novellas, and full-length novels. Some stories are inspired by real lives, some emerge from the long river of history, and others are born from the author's lifelong reflections on human nature, destiny, and the age in which we live. Here, tenderness exists alongside cruelty. Splendor walks hand in hand with tragedy. Dreams bloom, yet darkness is never far away. May these stories offer not only the pleasure of reading, but also a deeper understanding of the human world—and leave you with thoughts that linger long after the final page.
